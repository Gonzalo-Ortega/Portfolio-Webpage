Academic projects

I am currently working with Dr Ximena Fernández on research in Algebraic Topology and Topological Data Analysis (TDA), with a particular focus on fundamental‑group–based approaches to persistence. 

Here is a list of my previous literature reviews and academic projects completed during my Bachelor's and Master's studies:

  • Structure and Stability Theorems in Topological Data Analysis (2025) - Master's thesis, Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, under the supervision of Manuel M. Cuerno.
  • Optimal Transport for Topological Data Analysis (2025) - Final project for the course "Mathematic Analysis Fundamentals" thought by Fernando Soria, Universidad Autónoma de Madrid.
  • Introducción a la Topología Algebraica - El grupo fundamental y homología simplicial (2024) - Bacherlor's thesis, Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, under the supervision of Professor Pedro J. Chocano.
  • Métodos de Aprendizaje Automático para la clasificación de comportamientos estereotipados (2024) - Bacherlor's thesis, Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, under the supervision of Pablo Jercog Team and Professor Alberto Fernández Gil.
