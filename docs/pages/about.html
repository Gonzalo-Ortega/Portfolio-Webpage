About me

Hello! I'm a first year Mathematics PhD student at City St Georges, University of London under the supervision of Dr. Ximena Fernández.

My research interests include Algebraic Topology and Topological Data Analysis (TDA). I am currently working on developing alternative methods to study persistent invariants of filtered spaces beyond classical homology, with a focus on computational techniques involving the fundamental group and its presentations.

I completed my Bachelor's in Mathematics and Software Engineering in Universidad Rey Juan Carlos and my Master's in Mathematics and Applications in Universidad Autónoma de Madrid. I also spent one term on an Erasmus exchange at LUT University in Finland, and completed a six-month internship in the Pablo Jercog Team Neuroscience Reserch Lab at the Instituto Cajal (CSIC) in Madrid.
