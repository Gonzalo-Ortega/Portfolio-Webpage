About me

Hello! I'm a first year Mathematics PhD student at City St Georges, University of London under the supervision of Dr. Ximena Fernandez.

My research interests include Algebraic Topology and Topological Data Analysis. We are currently working on alternative methods to study the persistence of spaces beyond the use of homology, working with computations with the fundamental group and its presentations.

I studied my Bachelor's in Mathematics and Software Engineering in Universidad Rey Juan Carlos and my Master's in Mathematics and Applications in Universidad Autónoma de Madrid . I also stayed a term in an Erasmus program at LUT University in Finland, and made a six month internship at Pablo Jercog Team neuroscience reserch lab in Instituto Cajal (CSIC) in Madrid.
