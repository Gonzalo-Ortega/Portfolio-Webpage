About me

Hello! I'm a first year Mathematics PHD student at City St Georges, University of London under the supervision of Dr. Ximena Fernández. My research interests include Algebraic Topology and Topological Data Analysis. We are currently working on alternative methods to study the persistence of spaces beyond the use of homology, working mainly with computations over the fundamental group and its presentations.

I studied my Bachelor's in Math and Software engeneering in Universidad Rey Juan Carlos and my Master's in Mathematics and Applications in Universidad Autónoma de Madrid. I am also a self-thought indie game developer, having take part in several online world-wide game jams.
